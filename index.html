Install on iPhone

To add Tally to your home screen: tap the Share button in Safari, then choose Add to Home Screen. It will open full-screen with camera access, just like a native app.

All your data is stored locally on your device in localStorage. Nothing is ever uploaded except food images you explicitly scan.

About

Tally uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for BMR and applies Compendium of Physical Activities MET values for burn-off estimates. Calorie values are typical figures for planning — not lab measurements.

AI food identification is powered by Claude. The goal system is entirely goal-neutral: Tally helps you stay consistent with whatever you decide, not what anyone thinks you should decide.

Reset everything?

This will delete your profile and all logged items. You'll go through setup again. This can't be undone.
